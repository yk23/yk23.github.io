I am currently working as a postdoctoral research fellow at Brigham and Women's Hospital / Harvard Medical School, where I work on problems related to the microbiome. I earned my Ph.D. in Applied Mathematics from MIT, under the supervision of Bonnie Berger. My mathematical interests lie at the intersection of Combinatorics, Statistics and Biology.
